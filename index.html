Audio

Crystal Clear Sound

SLS owns a wide variety of audio reinforcement systems, otherwise known as speakers. Our compact QSC K12 series are great for small to moderate presentations, poetry nights, and small open mic nights. Need a little more coverage and volume? Our QSC K10 series will fit the bill just right. These are a great party speaker and are good for small dances and musical acts. For larger needs such as concerts and serious dance partiers we own a full JBL VRX900 Series Line array that will have your audience dancing and singing the night away.

If you are unsure what would work best for your event or need some customization mention in your service request that you would like some assistance and one of our technicians will gladly meet with you to ensure your event sounds just how you imagined at a price that fits your budget.

Want to pump up the audio for your event? Let us know by submitting a request here, and we will be happy to help you.

Lighting

Beautiful Light in Every Hue

SLS owns a wide array of lighting equipment suitable to all types of events. We have fully automated and controllable lighting rigs that give each and every moment of your event an energized feeling.

As always, if you are unsure what would work best for your event or need some customization mention in your service request that you would like some assistance and one of our technicians will gladly meet with you to ensure your event is lit just how you imagined at a price that fits your budget.

Power Distribution

Power for all occations

Need power? We have it, and a lot of it. For events large and small that need more power that the room provides our professional distribution center is able to fill your needs. Additionally, we have an extensive history and knowledge working with mobile generators and high amperage circuits. With the ability to run power hundreds of feet you can be sure your event will have the power it needs where it needs it.

If you need assistance determining the power needs for your event and how we can help you meet them, let us know in the service request form and one of our technicians will gladly assist you through the process of creating a power plan. We ensure sure you get what you need at a price within your budget.

Camera Rental

A free rental service to any student organization

SLS owns two professional level cameras that are available to all student organizations at no cost. All we ask for is a $20 deposit on the pickup of the camera which is returned when the camera is returned.

Our first camera is the Canon XA25: a professional line camcorder that is user friendly and will have you shooting great video in no time. With the optional addition of a professional grade wireless camera mic system you can be sure your organization’s videos will stand out from the pack.

Our second camera is an EOS Canon 7D Mark II Digital SLR. This camera is great for still photos and comes with all the necessary accessories to be a great video camera as well. No matter what your still photo needs are, the 7D Mk II will give you beautiful results, and as a bonus the camera is easy to use.

Need a professional level camera? Let us know by submitting a request here, and we will be happy to help you. Best part is: it's FREE!
